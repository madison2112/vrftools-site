About This Tool

The problem: Many email servers block .dat file attachments from being sent or received, making it difficult to share controller configurations with contractors, engineers, or clients.

The solution: Export any configuration as a .json file — email-friendly and fully reconstructable. Drop a .json here to restore the exact session it came from, including any group rearrangements. You can also use this to save your progress on a large site and pick up where you left off before the session expires.
